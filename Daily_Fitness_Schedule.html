Daily Fitness Schedule

Your 18-Minute Plank and Push-Up Plan

Morning Session (6 Minutes)

  • Plank: Hold for 2 minutes (straight or intervals).
  • Push-ups: Perform for 2 minutes (3 sets of 10-15 reps).
  • Rest: 2 minutes.

Afternoon Session (6 Minutes)

  • Plank: Hold for 3 minutes (straight or intervals).
  • Push-ups: Perform for 3 minutes (4 sets of 10-15 reps).

Evening Session (6 Minutes)

  • Plank: Hold for 2 minutes (straight or intervals).
  • Push-ups: Perform for 2 minutes (3 sets of 10-15 reps).
  • Rest: 2 minutes.

Remember to stay consistent and hydrate throughout the day!
